About

I’m a highly accomplished Entrepreneur, Music Business Executive, and Talent Manager with a wealth of experience in the entertainment industry. As the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Waveyard Entertainment, an independent record label based in Africa, Ghana.
I have been instrumental in the success of numerous musicians and artists. Throughout my illustrious career, I have worked with a diverse range of musicians and artists, including the likes of Benerl, Couture Music Group (USA), and my current artist, Niashun.

With an unwavering commitment to the success of my clients, I have earned a reputation as a highly skilled and effective talent manager who consistently delivers exceptional results. In addition to my work with Waveyard Entertainment, I have also established a consultancy firm, Ashbel Music Consult, which focuses on scouting, training, and mentoring talented individuals looking to enter the entertainment business.
