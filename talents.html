Overview

Niashun, a manifestation of art where he fuses African beat with modern twists, has made him an element of versatality where afrobeat, afrofusion, R&B, Hiphop, Reggae, Dancehall, country music and highlife all have an influence on his music. Niashun (born as Nana Kwame Ankumah) has a way with words that captivates his audience. His songs communicate love, life, and the struggles of the everyday people; his lyrics are relatable and thought-provoking which has made him a favorite among music lovers. Niashun's ultimate goal is to be a voice for the voiceless, the first name that comes to people's mind anytime, when they want to cruise and relax on a vibe.

Moreover, he wants to use his manifestation of art to bring people together, make them feel good and also inspire them. He believes that music has the power to unite people from different backgrounds and cultures. His unique sound and talent along with hardwork and dedication has made him a well sought after artiste in the music industry.

Overview

Niashun recently made his debut European tour in Switzerland, performing alongside Swiss music star Dodo and the Lioness of Africa, Wiyaala.

His music has been featured on several major DSPs such as Apple Music, Spotify, Youtube, which has garnered millions of streams.
